The Flipkart Mobile Brands Dataset contains information about smartphones available on the Flipkart platform. It includes details such as brand name, model name, selling price, storage capacity, and customer ratings. The dataset helps analyze smartphone market trends, customer preferences, and brand performance. Popular brands like Samsung, Apple, Xiaomi, OPPO, and realme are included in the analysis. Using this dataset, various visualizations and EDA techniques can identify top-performing brands, price distribution, and customer satisfaction levels. Overall, the dataset is useful for business analysis, market research, and understanding buying behavior in the online smartphone market.
